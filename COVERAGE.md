# Coverage

## What Is Included

- **Welfare standards** (64): Besluit houders van dieren, NVWA, EU transport regulation (1/2005), EU slaughter regulation (1099/2009), Beter Leven 1/2/3 ster criteria, SKAL biological standards, ingrepen (castratie, onthoornen, staartcouperen, snavelbehandeling), fixatie, euthanasie, ammoniakemissie/fijnstof, NVWA inspectie, IKB/KKM kwaliteitssystemen
- **Stocking densities** (42): Space requirements by species, age class, housing type, production system (regulier, biologisch, Beter Leven 1/2 ster), and transport densities
- **Feed requirements** (24): Energy (VEM/EW), protein, dry matter, minerals by species and production stage; ruwvoer-eisen, GMP+ certificering, soja-vrij/VLOG initiatieven, drinkwaterkwaliteit
- **Animal health** (39): Notifiable diseases (MKZ, varkenspest, vogelgriep, AVP, BSE, brucellose, tuberculose, rabies, miltvuur, kwade droes, Newcastle disease, bluetongue, Q-koorts); GD programmes (BVD-vrij, IBR-vrij, para-TBC, leptospirose, Maedi-Visna); vaccination policy (bluetongue BTV-3/8, Q-koorts geiten, Salmonella pluimvee, NCD); SDa antibiotic benchmarks per sector (melkvee, varkens, vleeskuikens, vleeskalveren); common conditions (mastitis, coccidiose, PRRS, rotkreupel, myxomatose, RHD, Schmallenberg)
- **Movement rules** (36): I&R identification and registration (RVO), oormerken, UBN-registratie, vervoersdocumenten, TRACES export/import, verzamelcentra, slachthuisaanvoer, VKI, R&O transportmiddelen, vervoersverbod bij dierziekte-uitbraak, geitenstop
- **Housing requirements** (21): Space, ventilation, flooring, temperature, lighting by species; includes biological, Beter Leven, vrije kraamstal, and aflammerruimte variants
- **Breeding guidance** (14): Gestation periods, breeding calendars, management advice, genomische selectie, KI, seizoensgebonden voortplanting
- **Fosfaatrechten**: Phosphate quota system for dairy farms

## What Is NOT Included

- **Paarden (horses)** -- separate welfare code, not yet ingested
- **Pelsdieren (fur animals)** -- banned in NL per 2024, not relevant
- **Real-time disease alerts** -- This is reference data, not a live alert system
- **Individual farm fosfaatrechten** -- Only the general system is described, not per-farm allocations
- **Provinciale verordeningen** -- Provincial rules (e.g. geitenstop) are mentioned but not exhaustively listed per province
- **Caribisch Nederland** -- Bonaire, Sint Eustatius, Saba have separate regulations

## Known Gaps

1. Movement rules in NL differ from UK -- the Netherlands does not have fixed standstill periods; instead, I&R reporting and disease-outbreak-based transport bans apply
2. Stocking densities for specialist or rare breeds may differ from standard guidance
3. Feed requirements are reference values -- actual needs vary by breed, condition, and climate
4. Antibiotic SDa benchmark values are updated annually -- check SDa website for current thresholds
5. Beter Leven criteria are summarized -- full criteria documents per species are maintained by Dierenbescherming
6. Rav ammonia emission codes are referenced but the full Rav-lijst is not included (>200 housing systems)
7. SKAL organic inspection details not included -- only the production standards
